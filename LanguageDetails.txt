SEPARATORS 	=	'(){}[],.:; sp(space)
OPERATORS 	=	*+-=/><%


Additional examples:
Valid IDENTIFIERS	:  	num, num1, large$, num$1, num2, num2$, a9, ab, ab2, etc...
